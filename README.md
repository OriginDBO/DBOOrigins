## 🌐 About DBO Unbound

DBO Unbound is a private Dragon Ball Online server project — a labor of love from a small, tight-knit team that got tired of waiting and decided to build something themselves.

We're not a massive organization. We're a small group of friends who care deeply about this game and the community around it. That's exactly what makes us different. Every decision, every line of code, every piece of content — it's made with intention, with care, and with our players in mind.

We started with nothing but publicly available resources and a shared drive to make progress where others hadn't. Over time, that turned into real achievements: custom skills with new animations and effects, new bosses, new systems, and content that hadn't been seen publicly since the original game went offline.

We've been at this since **April 2021** — and we're not going anywhere.

---

## 🎯 Our Purpose

The goal was simple from the start: **do what everyone's afraid of doing**

The DBO private server scene had no shortage of promises, but progress was slow, public, and rarely delivered. We chose a different path — heads down, focused, building in the background, and letting the work speak for itself.

We launched **DBOGenerations** in 2021 using the DBO TW 1.69 client as a base. Months later we shifted focus to the **DBO KR 2.0 client** — a significantly more complex undertaking — and began delivering things that hadn't been done publicly in the decade since the original servers shut down.

Some of what we're proud of:

- 🥋 **Brand new skills** for all classes — custom animations, effects, and sounds
- 🏃 **New character animations** — fly idle, fast fly, dash, dances, and more
- 👹 **New bosses** — completely original animations and skill sets
- 📦 **Custom content pipeline** — built and refined entirely in-house

This project — **DBO Unbound** — is the next chapter. A fresh start with everything we've learned, built the right way, with full control over our own codebase.
